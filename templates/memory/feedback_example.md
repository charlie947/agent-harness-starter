Never use the word "leverage" as a verb.

**Why:** It is jargon-y, vague, and signals AI-generated copy. Replace with "use" or a specific action verb.

**How to apply:** Whenever drafting any user-facing text (LinkedIn posts, emails, docs, marketing copy), scan for "leverage" and swap it out. Applies to all projects unless a project's `CLAUDE.md` overrides.
